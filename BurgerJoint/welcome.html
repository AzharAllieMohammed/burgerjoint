Our story

Chapter 1: The Humble Origins

It was the summer of 1965 when Joseph "Papa Joe" Marino, a passionate cook with a heart full of dreams, decided to turn his love for burgers into something more. Armed with his secret blend of spices and an unyielding determination, he opened the doors to his small eatery, Papa Joe's, in a charming corner of Rockwell. The menu was simple: handcrafted burgers that boasted an irresistible aroma that wafted through the streets, beckoning locals and visitors alike.
